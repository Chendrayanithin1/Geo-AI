# Evidence vs Causality in Earth Intelligence

Earth Intelligence systems often operate under uncertainty. Satellite observations provide evidence about the state of the environment but rarely provide direct information about causality.

A common mistake in environmental analysis is to assume that an observed signal directly implies a specific cause. In reality, a single observation can often be explained by multiple competing hypotheses.

## Observation vs Explanation

An observation is a measurable signal obtained from a data source.

Examples:

* NDVI = 0.22
* Distance to nearest lake = 300 meters
* NDVI decline of 15% compared to previous observations

These observations describe what has been detected.

An explanation attempts to answer why the observation occurred.

Examples:

* Vegetation stress
* Urban expansion
* Seasonal agricultural activity
* Land-use change
* Construction activity

Observations and explanations should not be treated as equivalent.

## Why Causality Is Difficult

Environmental systems are complex and influenced by multiple interacting factors.

A low NDVI value may be caused by:

* Water scarcity
* Urbanization
* Industrial development
* Seasonal variation
* Crop harvesting
* Natural barren terrain

Without additional evidence it is not possible to determine which explanation is correct.

## Principle of Multiple Working Hypotheses

For any environmental observation, multiple plausible explanations should be considered.

Example:

Observation:
NDVI = 0.22

Possible Explanations:

1. Vegetation stress
2. Urban expansion
3. Land conversion
4. Seasonal agricultural cycle

A robust Earth Intelligence system should surface competing explanations rather than committing prematurely to a single conclusion.

## Supporting and Contradicting Evidence

Evidence may support or weaken a hypothesis.

Example:

Observation:
NDVI = 0.22

Supporting evidence for water stress:

* Low vegetation health
* Large distance from water sources

Contradicting evidence:

* Nearby lakes
* Recent rainfall

The presence of contradicting evidence should reduce confidence.

## Design Principle

Earth Intelligence systems should focus on:

* Identifying observations
* Gathering supporting evidence
* Identifying contradicting evidence
* Ranking plausible explanations

rather than attempting to infer causality from a single signal.
